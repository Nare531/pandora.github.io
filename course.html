Courses we offer

At our film school, you’ll learn how to make movies, TV shows and become part of the entertainment industry.

PRODUCING
🎬

Discover how to turn ideas into movies and become a catalyst for creative projects through our film producing concentration.

FILM PRODUCTION 🎥

Earn your Bachelor of Science Degree in Film Production. As a student, you’ll be exposed to every stage of film production through a theoretical and gear-centric curriculum..

CINEMATOGRAPHY 📽

SCHOLARSHIPS AVAILABLE TO STUDENTS

The Pandora Film School is dedicated to the encouragement and development of future generations of media professionals.

PATHWAY SCHOLARSHIP

The Pathway Scholarship program will award up to $40,000 to students enrolled in campus-based bachelor’s degrees, and up to $20,000 for tuition costs to eligible students enrolled in campus-based associate degrees, over the course of their enrollment.

MOMENTUM SCHOLARSHIP

The Momentum Scholarship program will award up to $30,000 to students enrolled in online bachelor’s degrees, and up to $15,000 for tuition costs for eligible students enrolled in online associate degrees, over the course of their enrollment

PANDORA DREAMS SCHOLARSHIP

Purpose: Assist with tuition gap for need-based students in the program that do not qualify for PLUS or private loans. This scholarship is awarded only after all other funding options have been exhausted and is offered at the discretion of the institution.
